Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with me.

What types of projects do you work on?

I specialize in UX and Product Design for web and mobile applications. This includes user research, wireframing, prototyping, visual design, and design systems. I work with startups, enterprises, and everything in between.

What's your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A typical UX project takes 4-8 weeks, while larger product design initiatives can take 3-6 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work remotely?

Yes! I work with clients worldwide and am comfortable with remote collaboration. I use tools like Figma, Slack, and Zoom to ensure seamless communication throughout the project.

What's your design process?

My process typically includes: 1) Discovery & Research, 2) Strategy & Planning, 3) Design & Prototyping, and 4) Testing & Refinement. I tailor this approach based on your specific needs and constraints.
